Bitcoin is facing Quantum risk. and BitcoinQ is community driven quantum computing project. 5-10 years, 100-500 Bitcoins per year. Crowdfunding to develop a quantum computer. The goal is to upgrade approximately 4 million old Bitcoin addresses using P2PK (direct public key) to quantum-resistant addresses. 50%, or 2 million Bitcoin, will be charged as a technical service fee. 1Million Bitcoin will be airdropped to crowdfunding participants. 1Million Bitcoin will be used as administrative fees.
